About the project

This project was assigned to me in the Fall semester of my Junior year of college. I was assigned to make a booklet about two artists that we were given in class or pick two that I am inspired by and could compare and contrast. I chose two of my favorite cartoon show creators and made a booklet that I was extremly proud of. I used artwork that I found on their social media accounts and art books for the respected shows. This project helped me to learn layout skills and how to properly use a grid in design. I hope you enjoy my booklet!
