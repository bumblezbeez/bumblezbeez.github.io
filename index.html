About Me

My name is Sarena Letourneau and I love bees. So much so I made it the center of my brand. I am based in Seekonk Massachusetts, my home town. I am a graphic design major at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth, and I am also first generation college student in my family. I attended a vocational high school where I started learning the basics about graphic design. I have a variety of different pieces in graphic design, digital illustration, photography, and traditional drawings/paintings. Feel free as a bee to look at my work and contact me if you wish. Also check out my youtube channel where I sometimes post videos. I am currently looking for work.
